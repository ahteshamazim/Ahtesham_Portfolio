Transport

Transport such as bus and auto are available at reasonable rates. Admission to the school does not guarantee availability of bus and transport facilities. Depending upon the availability of seat and direction, transport facility may be provided. Withdrawal from bus facility must be preceded by a month’s notice in writing or a month’s fee in lieu of notice. Fare of the twelve months will have to be paid.
