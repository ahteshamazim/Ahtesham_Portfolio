Teaching

Modern methodology is followed for teaching language by direct method, Mathematics by practical process, Science by experiment, and Social Science by an integrated approach.

Minority Declaration

ST. Anne’s is a Minority Educational Institution declared by Government of India, National Commission of Minority Educational Institutions, New Delhi – 110 001

Medium of Instructions

Medium of Instruction in all subjects is English but equal emphasis is laid on the study and proficiency in Hindu & Sanskrit / Urdu as provided in the C.B.S.E. set up.

Music & Dance

“Taal” is the elementary thing in music and dance. Children who have good sense of “laya” or rhythm are taken into this. In the vocal taken into this class our main aim is to cultivate an ear for music. We train children to practice the basic notes of music.
